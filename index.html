How to play?

  1. Find all the isomers of the given compounds. There are 8 levels, each with a hidden time limit.
  2. Scoring:
    • Earn 60-100 points for each molecules found.
    • Lose 5 points for each duplicate, and 10 points for an incorrect one.
    • Earn 10 points for every 10 seconds early finishing before the time limit.
  3. The score is cummulative from level 1.
  4. The timer will count up from 0 for each level.
  5. The game interface is the white space. Hover over each button on top to see its function.
    1. To choose different atoms
    2. To clear canvas or delete one bond or atom
    3. To choose different bonds
  6. The found molecules are on the right, both 2D and 3D. Try rotating the 3D :)
